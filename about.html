ABOUT US

MEET THE OWNER

Larissa Finch

Owner and Operator of CANA Landscaping

A new mother to twins, Hanna and Carter, Larissa was inspired to pursue her interests in landscaping. Thus, the creation of CANA (Carter and Hanna's names combined) Landscaping had emerged from the great gloom of the pandemic. Experienced in landscaping design, business and customer service, Larissa creates easy to maintain,beautiful outdoor spaces, unique to each clients needs and wants. It's time to enjoy being outdoors again, it's time to enjoy your yard and garden!

SUPPORT LOCAL

All it took was a seed to be planted for Larissa to bring her dreams into a reality. Born and raised in Campbell River, Larissa grew up helping in the garden with her mom and brings a great understanding of the native flowers and trees that grow best in the region.

As a young woman entrepreneur, Larissa prides herself in supporting other women in their business ventures, from hiring a crew of strong females to promoting and encouraging fellow entrepreneurs through collaboration and community.
